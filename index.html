Our Story

A space to breathe

Founded in 2019 in the heart of Ocean Beach, Solé Yoga was born from a simple belief: that everyone deserves a place to disconnect from the noise and reconnect with themselves.

Our studio is more than a place to practice—it's a community. From sunrise flows to candlelit evenings, we create experiences that nurture body, mind, and spirit.
